Privacy Policy for Economic Revolution

Last updated: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Economic Revolution handles user information.
1. App Description
Economic Revolution is a personal finance tracking application that allows users to:

- track daily expenses
- analyze spending through visual charts
- manage budgets
- set financial goals
- monitor progress

The app is designed for personal use and works directly on the user’s device without requiring account creation.
2. How the App Works
The app allows users to manually input financial data such as expenses and categories.

All calculations, analytics, and visualizations are performed locally on the user’s device.

The app does not connect to any external servers for data storage or processing.
3. Data Collection
Economic Revolution does not collect or transmit personal data.
All user-entered data remains on the user’s device and is not accessible to the developer.
The developer has no access to any user data stored within the app.

The app:

- does not require registration
- does not request personal information such as name, email, or phone number
- does not create user accounts
4. Financial Data
Users may enter financial information such as expenses, categories, and goals.

This data:

- is used solely to provide app functionality
- remains on the user’s device
- is not shared with third parties
- is not transmitted to external servers
5. Local Storage
The app may store certain information locally on the user’s device, such as:

- expense records
- categories and budgets
- financial goals
- user preferences

This data:

- remains stored only on the device
- is not accessible by the developer
- is not shared externally
- is protected by standard device security mechanisms
6. Tracking and Analytics
Economic Revolution:

- does not use analytics tools
- does not use advertising frameworks
- does not track user behavior
- does not include third-party SDKs or external libraries that collect data
7. What the App Does Not Do
The app:

- does not require account creation
- does not send data to servers
- does not include advertising
- does not sell or share user data
- does not include social features (such as chat or comments)
8. Children
Economic Revolution is not specifically directed to children but may be used by a general audience.

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

The latest version will always include the updated date.

Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
